Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

App name: ER Taxi Quiz No account required Offline audio included

Overview

ER Taxi Quiz is designed to work without creating an account. The app does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other direct personal details for normal use.

The app does not track you across apps or websites, and it does not sell user data.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store a few simple preferences locally on your device to improve your experience. This can include:

  • Audio preferences
  • Map preference
  • Quiz progress

These settings are stored on the device itself and are used only to support app features.

Audio and Offline Use

The app includes embedded English audio files that work offline. During normal use, the app does not call Google Text-to-Speech.

Maps

When you choose to open a location in maps, the app may open Apple Maps or Google Maps depending on the preference you selected. These are third-party services and their use is subject to their own privacy policies and terms.
